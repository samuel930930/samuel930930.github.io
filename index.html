Samuel Chun Pong Lau

I am currently a third-year Computer Science Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence for Engineering and Medicine Lab (AIEM), Johns Hopkins University (JHU), supervised by Prof. Rama Chellappa. Before joining JHU, I received the B.Sc. degree in Mathematics at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2016, the M.Phil. degree in Mathematics at CUHK in 2018 and M.S. degree in Applied Mathematics at University of Maryland, College Park in 2020.

My research interests focus on Computer Vision and Deep Learning, especially Image Restoration, Face Recognition and Adversarial Robustness.
