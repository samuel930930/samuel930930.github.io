Samuel Chun Pong Lau

I am currently a MINDS Postdoctoral Fellow in Mathematical Institute for Data Science (MINDS), Johns Hopkins University (JHU), supervised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Rama Chellappa. I have received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, under the supervision of Prof. Rama Chellappa in 2021. I received the B.Sc. degree in Mathematics at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2016, the M.Phil. degree in Mathematics at CUHK in 2018 and M.S. degree in Applied Mathematics at University of Maryland, College Park in 2020.

My research interests focus on Computer Vision and Deep Learning, especially Image Restoration, Face Recognition and Adversarial Robustness.
